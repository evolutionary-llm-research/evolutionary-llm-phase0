**Diagnostic prompt:**
Summarize the key mechanisms by which misinformation spreads
in online environments and describe evidence-based interventions.

**Acceptance criterion:** std(h_x) < 0.1 and std(c_x) < 0.05 
across 5 runs with seeds [42, 123, 456, 789, 999].

combat misinformation include fact-checking, media literacy 
education, and algorithmic adjustments. Fact-checking involves 
verifying the accuracy of information and disseminating corrections. 
Media literacy education teaches individuals to critically evaluate

*Note: text ends mid-sentence at max_new_tokens=200 boundary.
This is expected and consistent across all runs.*

**Rationale:** Represents informational state of the model before 
any environmental exposure. I(X;seed) measures divergence from 
the pre-evolutionary baseline — direct operationalization of the 
panspermia analogy (drift from the original seed).

**Biological justification:** Analogous to measuring genetic distance 
from the ancestral genotype. If the model after N generations of 
toxin exposure produces outputs informationally distant from its 
pre-exposure state, I(X;seed) captures this drift directly.

## Evaluation criteria (mi_calibration.py)

Each seed will be evaluated across 4 MI implementations:
- mi_cosine (baseline)
- mi_entropy_decomp (current)  
- mi_jsd (1 - Jensen-Shannon Divergence)
- mi_npmi (Normalized Pointwise MI)

Selection criteria (in order of priority):
1. Correct direction: food > toxin for I(X;seed)
2. Effect size r food vs toxin (maximize |r|)
3. Within-class std (minimize — stability)
4. Monotonic gradient in LD50 (r < -0.8 with toxin concentration)

## Final Selection

Selected: Seed C + mi_token_ids_nmi
Date: 2026-05-08
Rationale:

- Strongest canonical separation: r=0.301 food vs toxin
- Biological justification: seed is base model output pre-exposure,
  operationalizes panspermia analogy directly
- Self-calibrating: seed regenerated per model, adapts to architecture
- Truncation artifact: verified negligible (delta H < 0.01, delta C < 0.001)
- LD50 gradient for I alone is flat (r=-0.564, p=0.188) but fitness
  gradient is strong (r=-0.931, p=0.002) driven by C(X) and H_dezorg
- mi_token_ids_nmi: NMI = MI / sqrt(H(X)*H(Y)), mathematically
  principled normalization, bounded [0,1]
